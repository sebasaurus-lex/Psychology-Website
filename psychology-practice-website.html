Our Story

A practice built on belonging

Willowbrook Psychology was born from a simple belief: that every person — regardless of age, background, or circumstance — deserves access to compassionate, high-quality mental health care.

Our team uses evidence-based approaches while always treating the whole person — not just the presenting concern. Whether you're 5 or 85, we meet you exactly where you are.

"Healing doesn't mean the damage never existed. It means the damage no longer controls your life."
— Our guiding philosophy

Our Approach

Tailored to you, not a template

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. Our clinicians draw from multiple evidence-based modalities — CBT, DBT, EMDR, psychodynamic therapy, play therapy — selecting the right tools for you.

Sessions are available in person at our Portland office or via secure telehealth for clients anywhere in Oregon or Washington.
